Frequently Asked Questions

Are my photos in this app public or private?

All your photos in this app can be seen by other members.

Will my name be visible to other members?

Your name, age, gender and location which you provided in the app will be visible to other members.

How many messages/likes can I send with premium membership?

You can send unlimited messages/likes during your premium membership.

How do I turn on/off Push notifications?

You can turn on/off all push notifications in your profile (Settings/My Account).

I think another user is sending me spam, what can I do?

Just hit the "Report as SPAM" button on the message. This sends us a notification telling us who has been reported and the message ID, we can then go and check the message. Users who send spam will have their profiles and all sent emails deleted without warning.

How can I search for members by username?

Currently, we are not offering members the ability to search by username. Our development team is working on improving the keyword search feature to produce more refined results. We greatly appreciate your patience in this matter!

Can I get a refund on my paid access fee?

Sorry, no refund can be provided on your paid access fees. If you would like to cancel your current subscription, you can find helpful information from Apple or Google Play

What kind of activity will lead to termination/block on my account?

If your account was suspended, a hold was placed due to suspicious account activity. Your profile and photos will be saved, but you will not be able to use the app, and other members will not be able to view your profile. Messages sent to your account will still be received, but cannot be accessed unless the suspension is fully reviewed and granted by Customer Support. Reasons for account suspension can include: Reports by other members about your profile or conduct Abusive, vulgar or sexually explicit language Asking for money up front or in advance of your date Using our site as an escort, or using the Service to solicit clients for an escort service Using our site to promote, solicit, or engage in prostitution Promoting or advertising a business Selling pictures, videos or cam sessions Soliciting passwords, bank information or other personal identifying information for commercial or unlawful purposes from other users Posting any false, misleading, or inaccurate content about yourself and/or your profile Having multiple active accounts Creating a profile if you are under the age of 18 Posting or sending material that exploits people under the age of 18 in a sexual or violent manner, or solicits personal information from anyone under 18 NOTE: We reserve the right to suspend accounts for any reason whatsoever, as outlined in our terms and conditions.

How do I close my account?

If you'd like to close your account just click on Edit Profile on your profile page, then click My Account, you'll find the option to close your account from there. All your photos and all sent messages will be deleted.

I have a question, how can I contact you?

You can contact us using a contact form here or send us an email at: support@travelmates.online. We will get back to you within 24 hours

What information will be shown on my credit card statement?

All payment processing information will appear as a Glad Technologies purchase and your anonymity will be 100% protected.
