If travel arrangments are made with a travel mate, then verify the user via a phone call, skype or webcam. / verify the travel mate via phone call, skype, and the web came before travel takes place.

Never send money to another member for any reason and report suspicious people to our support team.

Always update your Friend, Family, or guardian with all the relevant traveling information such as contact number, Address of the residing place, and travel itinerary.

Do not agree on terms where you are asked to be received or greeted by a third party.

Ensure you have sufficient funds in case you intend to end the travel before the agreed time.

Do your research before you travel to a new destination like local SIM update, Gather essential contact numbers(such as embassy, police, hospital, and local taxi), and keep someone updated all the time.
